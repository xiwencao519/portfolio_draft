Solution

产品功能

Product Features

游戏化 · 行动引导
Gamification · Action Design
任务种子 · 花园成长系统
将情感复原任务游戏化，完成任务推动花园植物生长，以视觉化进度代替抽象「自我提升」。

- AI 个性化推荐任务，而非固定菜单，与对话内容动态关联
- 任务极小化设计（5分钟内可完成），确保最低谷时也能启动
- 完成即触发植物生长动效，构建持续正向反馈循环
Task Seeds · Garden Growth System
Recovery tasks are gamified: completing tasks grows plants in the garden, turning abstract self-improvement into visible progress.

- AI-personalized tasks instead of static menus, dynamically linked to dialogue context
- Micro-task design (finishable within ~5 minutes) to lower activation threshold during emotional lows
- Immediate growth feedback after completion to reinforce a sustained positive loop
AI · 五阶段渐进引导
AI · Five-Stage Guidance
AI 情感对话系统
核心原则：把「给正确答案」转变为「让用户感到被理解」。基于心理学知识库 RAG 训练，五阶段策略精准控制对话节奏。

- 前几轮只做情感反映，不给建议，让用户先感到被听见
- 识别反复出现的情绪模式，帮用户命名与梳理核心痛点
- 情绪稳定后轻推极小行动，无缝衔接任务种子系统
- 实时评估情绪等级，Level 3 自动触发专业资源转介
AI Emotional Dialogue System
Core principle: shift from “giving the right answer” to “helping users feel understood.” A psychology-informed RAG base supports a five-stage conversational cadence.

- In early turns, the AI focuses on emotional reflection rather than advice
- It detects recurring emotional patterns and helps users name and structure core pain points
- After stabilization, it nudges micro-actions and hands off to the task-seed system
- Real-time emotional risk scoring triggers professional referral at Level 3
社区 · 被动共鸣优先
Community · Passive Resonance First
匿名内容社区
基于访谈洞察将「实时匹配」调整为只读优先的匿名故事社区——先让用户感到「有人和我一样」，再逐步降低主动发布门槛。

- 用「🕯️我懂」「🌱我后来好了」替代数字点赞，避免表演性内容
- 优先展示成长故事，以积极框架定义社区基调
- 发布默认匿名 + 限评 + 48小时消失；3秒冷静期减少冲动发布
- 风控原则：把「规则执行」转化为「关系关怀」
Anonymous Story Community
Based on interviews, “real-time matching” was replaced by a read-first anonymous story feed: users first feel “I am not alone,” then gradually gain confidence to post.

- Replace numeric likes with “I understand” / “I got better” reactions to reduce performative content
- Prioritize recovery narratives to establish a positive community frame
- Default anonymous posting + limited comments + 48h auto-expiry; 3-second cool-down for impulsive posts
- Risk governance principle: turn rule enforcement into relationship-centered care
